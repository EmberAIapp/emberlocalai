100% local AI

Everything stays with you.

Your notes, your files, your AI — all of it lives on your Mac. The model, the memory and the conversations never cross your machine's edge. No cloud to trust, because there isn't one.

Editable memory

It remembers. You decide.

Ember files what it learns about you as clear, plain-language facts — that you can read, edit or delete. Your AI knows you, and you keep your hand on everything it knows.

Built for privacy

What's yours stays yours.

Ember runs on your Mac, period. Your AI, your memory and your conversations stay on-device. The only thing that can reach the cloud is the optional work agent — and only when you approve it, action by action.
